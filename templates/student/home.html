Laboratory Rules and Regulations

To avoid embarrassment and maintain camaraderie at our laboratories, please observe the following:

  1. Maintain silence, proper decorum, and discipline inside the Laboratory. Mobile phones and other devices must be switched off.
  2. Games are not allowed inside the lab, including computer-related games.
  3. Surfing the Internet is allowed only with the permission of the instructor.
  4. Getting access to websites not related to the course is strictly prohibited.
  5. Deleting computer files and changing system setup is a major offense.
  6. Only officially enrolled students are allowed to use the laboratory.
  7. Sit-in students must have a valid reason for using the computer lab.
  8. Eating, drinking, and smoking are strictly prohibited inside.
  9. Clean up your workstation before leaving the laboratory.
  10. Report any damaged equipment to the laboratory administrator immediately.
